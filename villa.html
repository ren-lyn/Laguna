Villa Escudero Plantations and Resort

Experience Timeless Charm at Villa Escudero Plantations and Resort


Where Heritage, Culture, and Nature Come Together in Laguna! Step into a world of history and tranquility at Villa Escudero Plantations and Resort—a unique Filipino destination that blends rustic elegance with cultural richness. Located in the picturesque province of Laguna, this iconic resort invites you to experience the beauty of rural life and the warmth of traditional Filipino hospitality. Marvel at the famous Waterfall Restaurant, where you can enjoy authentic Filipino cuisine with your feet dipped in cool, flowing spring water—an unforgettable dining experience like no other! Explore the sprawling coconut plantation, paddle a bamboo raft across the serene Labasin Lake, or relax in native-style accommodations that transport you back to a simpler time. Don't miss the world-renowned cultural show featuring vibrant folk dances and music performed by the talented local community. Visit the on-site museum housed in a replica of a Spanish-era church, showcasing an extraordinary collection of religious art, historical artifacts, and Filipino heritage. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, a cultural immersion, or a fun family getaway, Villa Escudero offers a one-of-a-kind escape into the heart of the Philippines. Rediscover Filipino heritage, nature, and relaxation at Villa Escudero—your next memorable journey awaits!
