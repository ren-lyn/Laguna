Japanese Garden in Cavinti

Discover Serenity at the Japanese Garden in Cavinti, Laguna


Where History, Nature, and Tranquility Unite Escape to the Japanese Garden in Cavinti, Laguna—a peaceful sanctuary overlooking the scenic Lake Caliraya. Built in the 1970s by the Japanese government, this memorial park honors Japanese soldiers who perished during World War II and stands as a symbol of reconciliation and friendship between Japan and the Philippines . Wander through manicured landscapes featuring torii-style bridges, stone pathways, and a marble shrine nestled atop a hill. The garden’s tranquil ambiance, shaded trails, and panoramic lake views make it an ideal spot for reflection, picnics, and photography. Open daily from 7:00 AM to 4:30 PM, the garden welcomes visitors for a modest entrance fee of ₱20, which contributes to its upkeep . Experience the harmony of history and nature—plan your visit to the Japanese Garden today.
