Mount Makiling

Conquer the Mystical Mount Makiling


Hike Into Nature, Legend, and Adventure in Laguna! Towering over the provinces of Laguna and Batangas, Mount Makiling is a legendary and lush mountain that offers an unforgettable hiking experience for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and cultural explorers alike. Shaped like a reclining woman—believed to be the mythical guardian Maria Makiling—this dormant volcano is rich in biodiversity, mystery, and natural beauty. A favorite destination for hikers and researchers, Mount Makiling features well-maintained trails, dense rainforest, rare plant species, and diverse wildlife. The Makiling Trail to the summit (Peak 2) rewards trekkers with panoramic views of Laguna de Bay, nearby mountains, and the surrounding countryside. Along the way, you'll encounter natural hot springs, bubbling mud springs, and peaceful forest zones ideal for reflection and exploration. As part of the Makiling Forest Reserve, it also serves as a living laboratory managed by the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB)—perfect for educational eco-trips and environmental tours. Experience the wonder, challenge, and legend of Mount Makiling—where adventure meets the mystical heart of Laguna.
