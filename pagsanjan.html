Pagsanjan Falls

Discover the Majestic Pagsanjan Falls Your Next Great Adventure Awaits in Laguna!


Escape into nature and experience the breathtaking beauty of Pagsanjan Falls—one of the Philippines’ most iconic natural wonders! Nestled in the heart of Laguna, this towering waterfall is famous for its thrilling boat ride through lush, jungle-lined rivers and dramatic rock formations. Locally known as Magdapio Falls, Pagsanjan Falls offers more than just a scenic view. Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping “shooting the rapids” adventure as skilled boatmen guide you upstream in traditional wooden canoes, navigating through roaring currents and serene pools. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, nature lover, or simply looking for a refreshing getaway, Pagsanjan Falls is a must-visit destination. Enjoy swimming in the cool basin, passing under the falls, or exploring hidden caves behind the cascade. Plan your visit today and uncover the wild beauty and unforgettable adventure that only Pagsanjan Falls can offer!
