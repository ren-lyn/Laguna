Laresio Lakeside Resort

Dive into Adventure at Laresio Lakeside Resort in Los Baños, Laguna


Where Thrills, Nature, and Relaxation Unite Escape to the perfect mix of thrill and tranquility at Laresio Lakeside Resort in Los Baños, Laguna—an adventure-filled destination nestled beside the scenic Tadlac Lake. Famous for its heart-pounding activities like cliff diving, zipline jumps, Slip ‘N Fly slides, and The Blob, Laresio also offers serene moments with hot spring pools, lakeside views, and glamping under the stars. Whether you're kayaking across the calm waters, enjoying a bonfire night at Camp Q, or relaxing in cozy villas and cabanas, Laresio delivers an unforgettable getaway for adrenaline junkies and nature lovers alike.
