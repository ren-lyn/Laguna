Taytay Falls

Refresh and Reconnect at Taytay Falls


Nature’s Coolest Secret in Majayjay, Laguna! Looking for a serene escape from the heat and the hustle? Taytay Falls, also known as Imelda Falls, is a hidden paradise tucked away in the quiet town of Majayjay, Laguna. Famous for its crystal-clear, ice-cold waters flowing straight from Mt. Banahaw, Taytay Falls is the perfect retreat for campers, hikers, and anyone craving a refreshing dip in nature’s pool. Surrounded by thick forest and fresh mountain air, Taytay Falls offers a calm and peaceful ambiance—ideal for picnics, swimming, and nature walks. The short trek to the falls is easy and enjoyable, making it great for families and first-time adventurers. With its untouched beauty and cool, cascading waters, Taytay Falls is a must-visit destination for those seeking relaxation, natural beauty, and a break from the ordinary. Discover the chill of Taytay Falls—a simple yet unforgettable nature experience in Laguna!
