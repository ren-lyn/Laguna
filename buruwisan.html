Buruwisan Falls

Chase the Adventure at Buruwisan Falls


An Outdoor Enthusiast’s Paradise in Siniloan, Laguna! Get ready to explore one of Laguna’s best-kept natural wonders—Buruwisan Falls, located in the lush rainforest of Mt. Romelo in Siniloan, Laguna. Standing approximately 50 meters high, this majestic waterfall is a favorite destination for hikers, campers, and thrill-seekers who crave the perfect blend of adventure and tranquility. The trek to Buruwisan is a scenic journey through rolling hills, muddy trails, and river crossings—rewarding you with the stunning sight and soothing sounds of the falls crashing into a cool, swimmable basin. The surrounding forest is rich in wildlife and perfect for photography, while the campsite near the falls makes it a top choice for overnight stays under the stars. Whether you’re a seasoned trekker or a first-time explorer, Buruwisan Falls offers a raw, unforgettable experience that captures the wild and untouched beauty of Laguna. Take the trail less traveled—Buruwisan Falls is calling!
